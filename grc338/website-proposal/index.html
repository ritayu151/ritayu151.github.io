Introduction

Bakeology

Bakeology is a small family run bakery located in Los Angeles. Their goal is to create delicious gourmet pastries and cakes, while keeping their prices affrodable, so that everyone can enjoy them. Their products range from bread, to pastries, to custom cakes for special events.

Target audience

The target audience would be members of the local community, regardless of age, who are interested in seeing what the bakery offers that particular week, as well as those who cannot stop by in person.

The webpage will help inform potential customers about what products are currently avaliable and about the history of the bakery. By supporting the baker, they would be supporting small. local busienss owners.

About

Here at Bakeology, we believe that everyone should be able to enjoy the treats they like on a whim! Our goal for the bakery is to provide our customers with gourmet pastries and cakes, at an affordable price. We have treats to satisfy your every craving. Whether you’re craving sweet, tart, or even savory, we have it all.

Bakeology is nestled in the heart of Monterey Park, in the 626 Area of Los Angeles, located at 407 S. Atlantic Blvd, Monterey Park, CA 91754. Owning a bakery has been a long-time dream of our family’s, and we finally made that dream a reality in 2015. We pride ourselves on creating a fun, social environment for all members of the community to grab a bite, study, or just hang out. These past 5 years have been incredible. From the bottom of our hearts, we thank you for your continued support.
